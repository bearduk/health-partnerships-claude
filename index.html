About us

A new kind of health partnership for the North Midlands

Keele Health Partners brings together health, academic, community, and business partners to tackle some of the biggest health challenges facing Staffordshire and the North Midlands. Our goal is to turn great ideas and research into real-world solutions that make a positive difference to people's lives.

By working together, we can speed up the journey from discovery to delivery — making healthcare more accessible and effective at supporting those in need.

KHP creates opportunities for joint funding, shared facilities, and large-scale programmes, building on Keele's research strengths and ensuring that what we do is not only excellent but also impactful — benefiting patients, communities, and the wider health and care system while supporting regional growth.

Our five civic impact goals

Equitable Access
Making sure the benefits of health and care research reach everyone in our communities.
Impactful Research & Innovation
Delivering high-quality, co-produced research with evidence-based innovation at the heart of care.
Accelerated Translation
Moving discoveries into practice quickly so they can be adopted across the system.
Workforce & Regional Prosperity
Developing skills, supporting clinical–academic careers, and growing the local economy through health innovation.
Sustainable Commercial Partnerships
Building new collaborations through working with sector-leading organisations.
Research areas

Where we focus our work

Our work aligns with the NHS Long Term Plan — shifting care closer to home, embracing digital technology, and focusing on prevention.

Supporting mental wellbeing through research, digital tools, and community engagement. We want to create practical solutions that help people at every stage of life — from early intervention through to long-term management and recovery.
Driving biomedical research — including neuroscience, pharmacology, and bioengineering — and turning discoveries into personalised, preventative care. We work closely with industry partners to bring innovations from bench to bedside.
Building on Keele's expertise in musculoskeletal health and primary care to find better ways to support ageing populations and manage chronic conditions in community settings — improving quality of life and reducing system pressure.
Using novel technology, data, AI, and digital platforms to improve care, empower self-management, and free up clinical time. We work closely with Keele's Digital Society Institute and local tech businesses to develop and deploy real-world solutions.
